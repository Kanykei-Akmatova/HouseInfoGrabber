Blackburn Hamlet

The neighbourhood of Blackburn Hamlet can be found in the city of Ottawa, Ontario, which is located in the Ottawa - Gatineau metropolitan area.

Transportation

Travelling by car is very easy in Blackburn Hamlet. It is straightforward to access a parking spot. In contrast, the public transit service in Blackburn Hamlet is not especially frequent. Nonetheless, the neighbourhood is served by a few bus lines, and most properties are very close to a bus stop. Most houses for sale in this part of the city are located in places that are not very well-suited for those who travel by foot since carrying out daily needs is challenging. In contrast, Blackburn Hamlet is reasonably appropriate for cycling as there are a large number of bike lanes.

Services

It is generally possible for property owners in Blackburn Hamlet to purchase their groceries by walking. Residents benefit from a few restaurants and coffee shops as well. With regards to education, it is a short walk to access daycares and schools from most houses for sale in Blackburn Hamlet.

Character

People who prefer a slower-paced environment will enjoy Blackburn Hamlet. It is easy to reach green spaces in the neighbourhood since there are approximately 10 of them nearby for residents to check out. This area is very quiet overall, as noise from the streets and other parts of the city is rarely an issue.

Housing

In Blackburn Hamlet, roughly 45% of buildings are townhouses, while large apartment buildings and single detached homes make up most of the remaining housing stock. This part of the city is primarily composed of four or more bedroom and three bedroom homes. Homeowners occupy about 70% of the units in Blackburn Hamlet and 30% are rented. This neighbourhood experienced its biggest building boom in the 1960s and 1970s, so most of the available properties are from this time period.
